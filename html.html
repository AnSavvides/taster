Congratulations on being accepted onto the Code First: Girls Edinburgh taster session.

This short 2 hour introductory course will help you understand and hopefully give you a taste of what it takes to put together a website. We will very briefly cover two of the languages used when building websites, HTML and CSS.

Hopefully the course will both upskill you for now, but also make you want to do the full Code First: Girls course that is coming soon!

Interspersed within the notes you will find these blue exercise boxes, check things off as you complete them and come back later to start where you left off.
